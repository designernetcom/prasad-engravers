About Us

We, as PRASAD ENGRAVERS PVT LTD. feels proud in introducing as a leading Manufacturer, exporter, supplier of CNC Engraving Dies, Laser marking services, Etched, Printed labels/nameplate with finest quality and services. Established in 1990. we have been creating epitome in manufacturing excellence since 30Years Based in pune, an industrial hub, We have access to superior technology and harness our know how in such a way that enables us to make or signage products and marking services to optimum quality and services. We manufacture a series of industrial and domestic products in Engraved, Etched, Printed, Anodized, Laser marked variety that includes almost all metals such as Alumininum, SS 304, SS 316, and Non metals such as Polycarbonate, PVC, Acrylic, Vinly etc... We have manufacturing unit at Bhosari and Chikhali industrial area in Pune. The unit is supported by a range of avant-grade machinery and equipments.
